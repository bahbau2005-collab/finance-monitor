# Icon Resources Recommendations

## Recommended Sources (Free & Elegant):

### 1. **Heroicons** (https://heroicons.com/)
Best for: Professional, minimal design
- **Dashboard**: `chart-bar` or `chart-pie`
- **Aset**: `briefcase` or `building-storefront`
- **Cash**: `wallet` or `currency-dollar`
- **Hutang & Piutang**: `hand-raised` or `user-group`

### 2. **Phosphor Icons** (https://phosphoricons.com/)
Best for: Modern, sophisticated look
- **Dashboard**: `chart-bar` or `pulse`
- **Aset**: `package` or `vault`
- **Cash**: `wallet` or `money`
- **Hutang & Piutang**: `handshake` or `users`

### 3. **Tabler Icons** (https://tabler.io/icons)
Best for: Comprehensive, consistent style
- **Dashboard**: `chart-line` or `chart-bar`
- **Aset**: `briefcase` or `box`
- **Cash**: `wallet` or `coins`
- **Hutang & Piutang**: `handshake-2` or `users-group`

### 4. **Feather Icons** (https://feathericons.com/)
Best for: Clean, simple design
- **Dashboard**: `bar-chart-2` or `pie-chart`
- **Aset**: `briefcase` or `package`
- **Cash**: `credit-card` or `dolly`
- **Hutang & Piutang**: `users` or `share-2`

## How to Use:
1. Choose a source above
2. Download 4 icons in PNG format (transparent background)
3. Save to: `frontend/src/assets/images/icons/`
4. Update `App.jsx` to use the new icons

## Download Format:
- Format: **PNG** (transparent background)
- Size: **24x24** or **32x32** pixels
- Color: Can use any color, code will apply CSS color styling
